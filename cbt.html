Minimizing and Maximazing

Minimizing and maximizing events or issues at the same time. Sometimes we can minimize our achievements as if those achievements were not important. At the same time, we might maximize our mistakes believing that they are extreme or excessive. We may have a general tendency to exaggerate the significance of negative information and experiences while trivializing the significance of the positive.
